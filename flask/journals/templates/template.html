Journal Access Lookup

Please enter a list of journal titles, separated by commas or one per line; this app will return a CSV containing the journal titles and their OA status. Note that this will take a moment to process!

A preprint is the document submitted to a journal for publication consideration; this document becomes a postprint when it is ready for final submission, after peer review and additional edits.

  • OA Yellow - preprints may be posted publicly
  • OA Blue - postprints may be posted publicly
  • OA Green - preprints and postprints may be posted publicly
  • OA Gold - final published articles may be posted publicly
  • OA White - nothing may be posted publicly
