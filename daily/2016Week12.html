== Dough Recipe

If I have at least three days I will use a simple dough recipe (flour, water, salt, yeast) with about 65% hydration

Slow fermentation in the fridge, in individual dough balls

or overnight or less, I add oil and sometimes sugar to the dough and then let it rise once in bulk before a second rise as individual dough balls

Per pizza 110-125g water, 160-200g flour, 3g salt

Yeast depending on the desired rise time

But generally maybe 0.5g per pizza or less

For 6-8 pizzas and an overnight rise I would maybe use 1/2 tsp
