https://www.glidera.io/

Purse.io, an escrow service that enables users to purchase Bitcoin (anonymously, but above market rate) by purchasing items from Amazon (at a discount) from another user's wishlist.

Basically, you load your account with some bitcoin, link an Amazon wishlist, and set your desired discount

If a user is satisfied with the conversion rate you're offering, they accept your order and purchase the stuff to be shipped to you.

Purse.io locks the proportional amount of bitcoin in your wallet.  It plugs into Amazon, so it knows when the order has successfully been shipped.

Once you are satisfied that you are going to get your stuff (Amazon order ID + tracking number, or just wait until it arrives), you release the bitcoin to the purchaser.

at 03:00, I selected a 20% discount for a $150 order containing clothes, tools, and electronics

I thought 20% sounded like a pretty high discount, but my order was filled by the time I woke up this morning!

Besides the escrow system, the site also features a reputation system and limits the number and size of orders that a user can handle until they have built up a reputation.

Granted I just discovered this last night and haven't received my order yet, but there's an affiliate link: https://purse.io/?_r=UsAjdB

(affiliate link offer: we both received 0.01BTC, ~$6, after you transact for >$50)

it also plugs into Coinbase.com for direct purchasing of bitcoin with a bank account

and Shapeshift.io for on-the-spot conversion of any other cryptocurrency to bitcoin

and of course you can just transfer bitcoin in from any desktop or mobile wallet that you maintain.
