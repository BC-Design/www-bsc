Ally Bank Policy on Aggregators

Via: https://www.ally.com/resources/pdf/bank/ally-bank-obsa-02-26-2016-v9.pdf

"Aggregation Service" means an online account aggregation or personal financial management

service that retrieves, consolidates, organizes, and presents your accounts for the purpose of

allowing you to view your accounts with an Aggregator in a single location. An Aggregation

Service does not include a personal financial management and account aggregation feature or

service that we may offer through Online or Mobile Banking.

"Aggregator" means a Third Party who provides an Aggregation Service. An Aggregator does not

include a Third-Party that we retain to assist us in providing a personal financial management or

account aggregate feature that we may offer through Online or Mobile Banking.

B. Aggregation Services

If you choose to share your Password and/or Account Information with an Aggregator or other thirdparty

you acknowledge and agree that (i) we are not responsible for helping the Aggregator or thirdparty

in any way and will not be responsible for the information retrieved by the Aggregator or thirdparty;

(ii) we will not be responsible to you for any losses that may result from you sharing your

Password, using the Aggregation Service; (iii) you are responsible to review the security and privacy

standards of the Aggregator or third-party and to determine what your liability will be in connection

with the Aggregation Service; (iv) you will change your Password immediately when you end the

Aggregation Service; and (v) we have the right to prevent Aggregators or third-parties from accessing

your Accounts.

4. Berkeley Cleanweb Kickoff And Coffee

3pm - 4pm , Wednesday, March 9th, D-Lab 356 Barrows Hall

Berkeley Cleanweb returns! Join us for some mid-afternoon coffee and pastries, hear about some awesome projects, and kick back with some fellow data nerds. If you are working (or planning to work) with data in energy in any context, whether you're a complete data novice or a seasoned Python veteran, we'd love to see you there! We will introduce Cleanweb's basic activities, including projects, skill sessions, and speaker events, as well as open the floor to your suggestions as to how Cleanweb can best serve you this semester. We'll also have a short presentation on the European Electricity Price Forecasting competition, which some cleanwebbers are participating in. Checkout http://cleanweb.berkeley.edu for more information, and to join our Google group to stay up to date!
