1. BERC Innovative Solution (BIS) Info Session

12:00pm Friday, January 29, 2016  - Haas School of Business - Room TBD

12:00pm Tuesday, February 2, 2016  - Sutardja Dai Hall - Room 250

The Spring semester of BERC Innovative Solutions (BIS), a student run consulting initiative, will be starting shortly. BIS allows students to work on real-world consulting projects with external clients ranging from cleantech firms to state and local government. The BIS leadership team will be holding an info session on January 29.  It is mandatory that you attend an information session if you would like to participate (note: Haas students must attend this info session due to the add/drop schedule). There will also be a second info session on February 2nd. If you are absolutely unable to attend either info session, but would like to participate, please email: bis@berc.berkeley.edu.

At the info session, the BIS Chairs will outline all of the companies/projects the students have the opportunity to work with. Following the info session, students will rank order their preferences. Final teams will be announced by February 5th.

Lunch will be provided, please RSVP to ensure there is enough food.
