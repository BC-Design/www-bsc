## Wallet Contracts vs Accounts

When deployed with `n` owners, wallet contracts are multisignature vaults that require `m` of `n` owners to sign transactions that exceed a daily threshold.

* [/r/ethereum: What's the difference between account and wallet contract?](https://www.reddit.com/r/ethereum/comments/45xeiw/whats_the_difference_between_account_and_wallet/?st=irkkzssp&sh=789d9918)

## Transacting from the Geth Interpreter

The Mist wallet provides a graphical interface for creating and managing a wallet contract, but wallet contracts can also be managed from the Geth command line interpreter.  The process of interacting with a contract is described in general terms in [the Ethereum Homestead docs](http://ethdocs.org/en/latest/contracts-and-transactions/contracts.html#interacting-with-a-contract).
