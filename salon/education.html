Question for those in the know regarding MOOCs. Is there any software out there to handle grading in a distributed, crowd-sourced fashion?

bgillespie
2015-10-16 23:32
In particular I have an idea for a system that would be useful as a way to provide feedback to students in a course without large-scale intervention by instructors:

bgillespie
2015-10-16 23:35
Students scan their assignment (or otherwise submit a PDF), and then students are randomly matched with other students' assignments (at least 2) and asked to grade the assignment. Solutions are provided for reference at this point. Students receive a completion score for grading their assigned submissions, and also get the educational feedback of seeing and assessing other students' work in the process.

bgillespie
2015-10-16 23:39
To provide some level of accountability for the grades being given, if two grades for an assignment differ by some threshold amount, then a flag is raised and an instructor takes a look. If there is a problem with the grade given by one or more of the students, said students lose their grading completion score and perhaps a few points more. The instructor can provide a definitive grade in this case.

bgillespie
2015-10-16 23:41
PDF annotation capability would be a nice touch.
