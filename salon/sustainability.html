bpl
2015-09-23 01:17
http://www3.epa.gov/blackcarbon/basic.html EPA: On road and Off Road diesel are tied at about 40% each for Mobile Carbon Soot Emission (not Carbon Dioxide Emissions). The total category makes up about 50% of US emissions and the next largest category is "open biomass burning, including wildfires" at ~35%. Total US emissions are expected to decline sharply due to increase vehicle/heavyequipment emission standards. This is mostly going to be the result of better capture and burn equipment on diesel trucks which, while a large source than diesel cars due to sheer number, are larger and easier to implement pollution controls on.
