seekarete
2015-09-23 22:52
Depends on the time of year and reason for the test. Some schools require a PT score to apply/attend. For normal guys, if you fail multiple tests in you can be kicked out within maybe 3 months. It's a matter of command motivation and paperwork. If they're about to deploy or you demonstrate improvement, they'll be more lenient. If you have rank/influence you can avoid being tested for years.

seekarete
2015-09-23 22:53
By regulation, you only need one passing PT score per year, but some units do it more frequently.
